Hello!

I'm Richard and I'm a full-stack developer in San Francisco who loves finding the solution to the "how". I dove into software development because I wanted to challenge myself and I love solving puzzles. I see coding as building a solution to a puzzle, and it's the fun in figuring out how to arrive to it. I grew up being immersed in how technology evolved from something like blocky cellphones to smartphones or 32-bit video games to virtual reality gaming. However, instead of simply being just fascinated by it, I'm striving to be apart of it.

I bring with me a skillset in JavaScript, React, PostgreSQL, HTML5, and CSS3 with a hunger to learn more. When I'm not on the grind, I love reading comics and appreciating people's artwork. I also love playing games in all forms whether it be tabletop or video. View my latest projects below and more on my Github profile.
